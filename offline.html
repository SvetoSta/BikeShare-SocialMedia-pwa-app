Our goals here at PikaBike:

  • Creating a biking app, which aims at securty and safty on the road.

  • Help others

During our day and age, we have seen that a lot of people do not feel safe while being outside. They are afrade of being kidnapped or attacked. There are apps that offer transportation vichecules, but do not offer the protection that some people want and need. This is why PikaBike is here to have all the security in check and provide oyu with a pleasant and safe trip with a bike.
